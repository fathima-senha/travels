About Us

This travel agency website is built with HTML and Bootstrap. It has a clean and modern design. The navigation bar is placed at the top. It contains links to Home, Destinations, About, and Contact. The homepage includes a hero section. This section has a title and a call-to-action button. The button leads users to the destinations section. The destinations section shows nine travel spots. Each spot is displayed in a Bootstrap card. The card includes an image. The card also has a title. A short description is written under each title. A "Book Now" button is provided for every card. The images are taken from Unsplash. The destinations include Paris, Maldives, Tokyo, New York, Rome, Dubai, London, Sydney, and Bali. Each destination highlights unique attractions. The design is fully responsive. It works on mobiles, tablets, and desktops. The about section is simple and centered. It tells about the agency’s experience. It explains that the agency has more than twenty years of service. It also mentions personalized travel packages. The contact section includes a form. The form asks for name, email, and message. A send button is placed below the form. The footer is styled with a matching background. It contains copyright text. The overall design uses Bootstrap’s grid system. Buttons, forms, and cards are styled with Bootstrap classes. The website loads quickly. It is easy for beginners to edit. It looks professional, clean, and user-friendly.
